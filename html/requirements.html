Entering your requirements

Once you have worked with your users, designers, analysts and other important stakeholders and identified the requirements that will provide the components and functionality you need in the technology you are evaluating, it's time to document those requirements for scoring and further evaluation.

TSRM makes this easy by allowing you to quickly and effortlessly enter (and edit) requirements.

Requirements are the foundation of evaluating technology choices and entering them is the first step when using TSRM.
