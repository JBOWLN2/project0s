Weighting your requirements and groupings

Once you have identified your requirements and grouped them in to meaningful groupings, the next step is to applying weightings based on how important each requirement is to your selection. This will ensure the score of each piece of technology you are eveluating prioritizes the most important requirements.

TSRM makes this easy by allowing you to quickly plug in the weight of each requirement.
